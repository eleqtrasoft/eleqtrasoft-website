Graphic Designing

GGraphics Design is all around us! Words and pictures; the building blocks of graphics design- are the elements that carry the majority of the content in both the digital world and the printed world. As graphics design becomes more visible and prevalent in our lives, it has emerged as a new modern world skill that everyone must possess, graphics design as a practice has become very important in our work culture.

In this course, you’ll learn the theory behind creating attention-grabbing visuals and understand the basic principles and elements of designing graphics. Learn how to manipulate pixels to create extraordinary designs out of mundane images.
